About Us

Sigma Eta Pi at Miami University is a co-ed entrepreneurship fraternity of 27 members who learn by doing: shipping real products, talking to real customers, and generating real revenue long before we call something an idea. We are not a club that talks about entrepreneurship — we practice it. But the work is only half of it. We love the life around it too: the friendships, the late nights, the small wins celebrated loudly. SEPi is where the people who chart their own course find each other early, learn fast, take smart risks, and push each other to act.

Our Mission

Our mission at Miami SEPi is simple: get students starting early. Whether that means launching a venture or building the skills and network that carry a career, we remove the friction to begin, surround members with mentors and friends who won't let them coast, and measure success by attempts taken — and by who our people become along the way.
