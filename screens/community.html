Sigma Eta Pi was founded in 2010 at UCLA as the first co-ed entrepreneurship fraternity, built to transform students into starters who follow their passions, build lasting relationships, and create real value through innovation. Today, members across 11 chapters have raised more than $1.24B in funding and launched 100+ ventures.

About Us

Sigma Eta Pi at Miami University is an innovation factory. We take the most ambitious, creative, and driven students on campus, put them in a room together, and build the kind of people who go on to run great companies. We are not a club that talks about entrepreneurship. We learn it by doing: shipping real products, talking to real customers, and generating real revenue long before we call something an idea. But ambition is only half of it. We love the work and we love the life around it, the friendships, the late nights, the small wins celebrated loudly. SEPi is where the people who chart their own course find each other early, learn fast, take smart risks, and push each other to act.

Our Mission

Our mission at Miami SEPi is to turn ambitious students into founders and builders by removing every excuse not to start. We make building a company borderline frictionless, we surround our members with mentors and friends who refuse to let them coast, and we measure success not by status but by purpose, by attempts taken, and by who our people become along the way.
